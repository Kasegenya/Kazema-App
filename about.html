KAZEMA FINTAX SOLUTIONS

is a Tanzanian accountancy firm offering expert services in finance, accounting, auditing, tax, training, and consultancy. Its partners bring experience from leading firms such as Kasengenya & Company and PACE Associates Ltd

Kasengenya & Company (Certified Public Accountants in Tanzania), is one of the leading business advisory consulting, Auditing and accounting firms. The firm possesses a unique culture of providing services ethically and professionals with staff acquainted with professional qualifications, exposure and experiences.

The firm has built experience spanning industries and different economic sectors, which include but not limited to manufacturing, agricultural, tourism, and services, investment, public and donor funded organizations. It becomes first and the only firm in Tanzania as a member of Russell Bedford Global accounting network, with headquarters in London. With Russell Bedford International, the firm enjoys an access to a worldwide network of professional services from experienced personnel globally.

Our Identity

KAZEMA FINTAX SOLUTIONS is uniquely identified through its logo and colors. Our logo represents a symbol of solutions categorized into major three categories, which are professional practice in finance, accounting and auditing, legal aspects in tax matters, and training and consultancy in business matters.

Our three colours assure confidence to our clients for the services and solutions we provide, along with professional core values. The blue colour indicates our professional confidence, reliability and responsibility, the green pearl indicates our balance in providing services, growth and hope to us and our clients
